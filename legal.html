Privacy Policy

What Flashdown collects

Nothing. Flashdown runs entirely in your browser. The file you drop is read locally via JavaScript and never sent to any server. When you close the tab, everything is gone.

Third-party services

Flashdown loads a handful of open-source libraries from CDNs to do its work. These services may log standard request information (your IP address, browser agent, etc.) under their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare CDN (cdnjs.cloudflare.com) — serves the conversion libraries (marked, mammoth, turndown, pdf.js). Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) — serves the site's typefaces. Privacy Policy

No cookies are set by Flashdown itself. The site does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising.

Data retention

There is no database, no server-side storage, and no logging on our end. Your file and its converted Markdown exist only in your browser's memory for the duration of your session.

Your rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, or delete. If you have questions, reach out via the contact information below.
